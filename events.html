Rules

  1. A team should consist of a maximum of two members.
  2. Entries can be submitted in two categories: Technical & Non-Technical
  3. Entries must be submitted in a word document, as per the format given below.
  4. Entries will be shortlisted by a group of experts, prior to the competition.
    Shortlisted teams will be informed via e-mail.
  5. Shortlisted teams will need to register at the venue, at 9:30 AM.
    Registration fees are Rs. 200/- per idea presented.
    Members of the E-Cell need not pay the registration fee.
  6. The final presentation must be a PowerPoint presentation, and it must include the business model canvas.
  7. Five teams from each category will be shortlisted, to present at the competition.
  8. Time limit given should be adhered to. No extension shall be allowed.
  9. No foul language or aggressive actions allowed.
  10. Judging criteria will be decided by a panel of experts.


The final round of the competition will consist of the following stages: (On Stage)
Stage 1
A slide show using Microsoft PowerPoint, or equivalent program, should be presented.
  • Time limit: 7 minutes maximum.
  • No limit on number of slides
  • Either one, or both team members may present.
  • Stage 2 will commence as soon as Stage 1 concludes.
Stage 2
Q & A Round.
  • Time limit: 3 minutes.
  • Questions will be asked by Judges, Teachers, and Audience.
  • Judges' decision is final and binding.


Format for submission of entires:
Document Format Business Model Canvas
Entries are to be mailed to pccecell@gmail.com in Word Document or PDF format.
